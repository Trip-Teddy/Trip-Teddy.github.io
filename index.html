Problem

People often encounter difficulties on deciding where to go for a trip, and also planning the trip.

Solution

TripTeddy categorize the spots with clear and informative labels, introduce the “playlists” function for itinerary recommendation, and empower users to collaborate on the planning.

Key Features

Discover

Discover travel playlist based on the criteria that best suits you.

Plan

Duplicate travel playlist and edit to your heart's content.

Share

Share travel playlist with your friends and start your journey.
